Employment Management System Overview

Introduction

The Employment Management System (EMS) is a comprehensive application designed to facilitate the management of employees and departments within an organization. This system aims to streamline administrative tasks, improve data accuracy, and enhance overall operational efficiency by providing a centralized platform for managing employee records, departmental information, and related activities.

Key Features

1. Employee Management

- Employee Profiles: Create, update, and manage detailed employee profiles, including personal information, contact details, job roles, and employment history. - Role Management: Assign and update employee roles, including promotions and role changes. Support for different role types such as managers and staff. - Search and Filter: Quickly find employees using various search and filter criteria such as name, department, role, and employment status.

2. Department Management

- Department Profiles: Create and manage department profiles, including department name, location, and unique identifiers. - Department-Employee Association: Link employees to their respective departments and manage departmental hierarchies. - Manager Assignment: Assign managers to departments, ensuring that managerial roles are correctly attributed based on employee role criteria.

3. User Authentication and Authorization

- Secure Login: Ensure secure access to the system with user authentication. - Role-Based Access Control: Restrict access to certain features and data based on user roles (e.g., admin, manager, employee).

4. Data Integrity and Validation - Unique Constraints: Ensure data integrity with unique constraints on fields such as employee ID and department name. - Validation: Implement validation rules to ensure that all necessary data is accurately and consistently recorded.

5. API Endpoints

- CRUD Operations: Provide API endpoints for Create, Read, Update, and Delete (CRUD) operations on employees and departments. - Promotions: Dedicated endpoints for promoting employees and changing their roles within the organization.
